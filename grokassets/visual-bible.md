# Visual Bible — LojaSync

**Core Metaphor**: Practical, reliable Brazilian e-commerce and ERP automation tool. A clean, efficient machine for bulk product registration, romaneio (packing lists), nota fiscal, and grade consolidation in Byte Empresa. Desktop + web hybrid that reduces manual work for store owners. Professional operational tool, not flashy.

**Emotional Promise**: "I can process hundreds of products and documents reliably without the usual headache and errors."

**Visual DNA Keywords**: Clean operational efficiency, Brazilian retail reality, document handling, automation flows, practical business software aesthetic, trustworthy and no-nonsense.

**Color System**:
- Professional business: deep navy/indigo + clean grays + one confident teal or green for success/automation states.
- High trust, practical, never playful.

**Typography Direction**: Clean, highly readable sans. Professional and modern for business use.

**Audience & Cultural Notes**: Brazilian lojistas and e-commerce operators who deal with high-volume product management and fiscal documents daily. They value reliability and time savings.

**Forbidden Styles**:
- Cute or consumer startup look
- Heavy tech/cyber
- Playful gamification

## Reference Assets

Logo should feel like a solid, professional automation platform — perhaps a stylized document + gear or synchronized flow icon with clean typography.

Hero images: Clean workspace with multiple documents being processed automatically, web panel + desktop automation working in harmony.
